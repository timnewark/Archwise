Building Surveys in Cardiff, Bristol, Newport, Swansea

A level 1 survey, known as a Condition report, offers a basic overview of the property's state and is generally the most affordable type of survey available for property purchases.

A level 2 survey, known as a buyers report, is the most appropriate when buying anything but a new build property.

Learn more about Archwell Building Surveys

Level 1 house survey (Condition report)

A basic overview of the property's condition to identify any significant issues without going into detailed analysis.

Read More

Level 2 house survey (Buyer report)

Offers more detailed information than a Level 1 survey, focusing on identifying problems that could affect the property's value. It is more comprehensive and includes advice on repairs and maintenance.

Read More

Level 3 house survey (Building survey)

The most comprehensive type of survey, intended for older, larger, or non-traditional properties, or those that have been significantly altered or require major renovations.

Provides an in-depth analysis of the property's structure and condition.
