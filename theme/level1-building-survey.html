Level 1 building survey

A Level 1 building survey, also known as a condition report, is the simplest option among three different types of property surveys. It is designed for homeowners or potential buyers who require a neutral, expert evaluation of the state of a property.

This type of survey is most appropriate for properties that are newly built, adhere to standard construction methods, and do not feature any complex designs or significant wear and tear.

Our surveyor carries out a visual inspection without testing the building materials or systems directly. The resulting report does not provide recommendations for repairs or upkeep.

In instances where the surveyor is unable to make definitive assessments, the report will recommend additional investigations to fully comprehend the property's condition.
