Privacy Policy for Archwise

Feb 2nd 2024

Welcome to Archwise, your trusted partner in building surveying. Your privacy is of paramount importance to us.
This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect from our users, how we use it, and the steps we take to protect it.
By accessing or using our website and services, you agree to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this policy.

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. The types of personal information we may collect include:
• Contact Information: Name, email address, telephone number, and any other information you provide when you contact us or use our services.
• Survey Information: Details about the properties you are interested in surveying, including location, size, and specific requests or needs.
• Usage Data: Information on how you use our website, such as the pages you visit, time spent on pages, and other similar data.


How We Use Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including to:
• Provide, operate, and maintain our website and services.
• Improve, personalize, and expand our website and services.
• Understand and analyze how you use our website and services.
• Develop new products, services, features, and functionality.


Sharing Your Information

We do not share personal information with companies, organizations, or individuals outside of Archwise except in the following cases:

• With Your Consent:
We will share personal information with companies, organizations, or individuals outside of Archwise when we have your consent to do so.

• For Legal Reasons:
We will share personal information with companies, organizations, or individuals outside of Archwise if we have a good-faith belief that access, use, preservation, or disclosure of the information is reasonably necessary to meet any applicable law.

Security

The security of your personal information is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Other services

The range of other services offered by Archwise Building Surveying

Residential Valuations

Our methodology includes meticulous verification of data, encompassing title deed examination, property inspections, precise measurements, thorough due diligence, market analysis and the application of valuation models as needed.

Dilapidations Survey

Our Dilapidations Survey service meticulously evaluates your property to pinpoint any lease agreement non-compliances regarding upkeep and repairs, safeguarding both landlords and tenants from future legal issues and unexpected costs.

Reinstatement Cost Assessment

Our Reinstatement Cost Assessment accurately estimates the expenses to rebuild your property, ensuring your insurance coverage is precisely aligned with current construction costs and standards.

Party Wall Act

Our expert service navigates the Party Wall Act, facilitating agreements between neighbors on construction projects affecting shared walls or boundaries, ensuring compliance, preventing disputes, and protecting property interests for all parties involved.
